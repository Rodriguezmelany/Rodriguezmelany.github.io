A little about Me!

I am currently a Junior at the University of Massachussetts Amherst, studying Operations and Information Management, minoring in Information Technology. At UMass I have held the position of Vice President of Finance for the Sigma Delta Tau Sorority as well as the Diversity Equity and Inclusion Chair for two terms. Currently, I am part of the Isenberg Insurance Academy Fellowship. This fellowship which is a part of the business school, consists of building an academy and growing connections with businesses for future students to gain opportunities. This summer I am beginning an internship position as a Technology Risk Consulting Intern at Ernst and Young.
