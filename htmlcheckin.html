How to help Climate Change

The climate is rapidly changing and we need to take the right steps in helping our Earth.

What can we do to help climate change?

  • Save energy at hom, turn off lights, unplug appliances when you are not using them
  • Take public transportation, walk, or bike whenever you can
  • Eat a more vegetables, less meat results in less greenhouse gasses being emmitted
  • Throw away less food, it can lower your carbon footprint up to 300 kilograms of CO2e per year
  • Consider switching to an electric vehicle
